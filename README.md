\# Lawvable Web3 Compliance Auditor



A production-grade Web3 legal AI skill designed to transform large language models into structured crypto compliance and regulatory analysis assistants.



This repository contains a modular legal engineering framework for:

\- token classification analysis,

\- DAO governance review,

\- DeFi protocol compliance assessment,

\- MiCA readiness analysis,

\- FATF/AML signal detection,

\- smart contract legal alignment review,

\- and preliminary Web3 regulatory risk assessment.



\---



\# Disclaimer



This repository does not provide legal advice.



All outputs generated using this framework require review by qualified legal counsel licensed in the relevant jurisdiction.
